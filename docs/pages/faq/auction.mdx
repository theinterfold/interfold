# The Interfold Auction

The initial public distribution of FOLD will take place through a Uniswap Continuous Clearing
Auction (CCA). The auction is designed to distribute tokens progressively and establish a clearing
price through participant demand.

## What is a Uniswap Continuous Clearing Auction?

A Continuous Clearing Auction (CCA) is a price-discovery mechanism for token sales built on Uniswap
infrastructure.

- The auction runs across multiple discrete periods.
- Participants submit either a market order or a capped order that sets the maximum price they are
  willing to pay.
- At the end of each period, all successful participants pay the same clearing price.
- In the auction, the minimum price for each subsequent period is the greater of the original floor price or the previous period’s clearing price. As a result, the minimum price may rise in later auction periods, but it does not fall from one period to the next.
- Any tokens not distributed earlier remain available in the final period.

By distributing tokens progressively and applying a uniform price within each period, the CCA is
designed to reduce gas competition, prevent sniping, and allow clearing prices to develop through
participant demand. The result is intended to support a more transparent and orderly auction process for participants.

[Read more about Uniswap CCAs](https://cca.uniswap.org).

## Where can I participate in the FOLD token auction?

The FOLD token auction will be accessible through the Uniswap web app. The official auction page,
participation instructions, and relevant contract details will be published through Interfold's
verified channels before the auction opens.

## Who can participate in the FOLD token auction?

The auction is available to eligible participants, subject to jurisdiction, verification, AML/sanctions screening, and other applicable restrictions. Additional restrictions may apply based on jurisdiction, residence, citizenship, entity status, sanctions screening, or other legal requirements. Participation may be restricted in certain jurisdictions, including the UK. EU participation may be subject to limits of fewer than 149 persons per member state.

## Is there any cooldown period associated with FOLD purchased during the FOLD token auction?

Yes. Tokens purchased in the FOLD token auction are subject to a cooldown period of 40 days. During this period, general transfers are restricted, though FOLD may be used for ciphernode bonding.

After the cooldown period ends, general transferability is expected to begin.

## Do I need to complete KYC to participate?

Yes. All participants must complete KYC through our third-party provider, Predicate, to verify their jurisdiction and screen for AML/sanctions.

## How long will the FOLD token auction last?

The FOLD token auction will open with a 48-hour registration and verification window, followed by a 48-hour bidding window.
