## Community Allocations

### Foundation Treasury

The Interfold has been built in the open and is freely available as open-source software under the
LGPLv3 license. A portion of supply is allocated to The Interfold Foundation. Grants may be awarded proactively to support priority work or
retroactively to recognize meaningful contributions.

### CCA

The CCA is designed to provide open access through uniform clearing mechanics. The starting fully diluted valuation will be set at $20M. This is simply the starting price for the auction and does not represent any projection or estimate of FOLD’s actual price or value.

### Airdrop

This allocation is reserved for eligible community participants and ecosystem contributors.
Distribution criteria, eligibility requirements, and timing will be announced separately.

### Liquidity Reserve

Reserved to support market liquidity, primarily through decentralised exchange liquidity pools, and
to help maintain orderly trading conditions following launch.

## Other

### Gnosis Guild

The Interfold was initially incubated by Gnosis Guild, a venture studio. This allocation recognises Gnosis Guild's early role and is subject to a 48-month
release schedule.

### Investors

The Interfold (previously developed under the name Enclave) has completed two previous funding
rounds: pre-seed and seed. Investors in these rounds share a common vision of sustainable,
distributed privacy infrastructure systems. Previous investor and Legion participant terms are being updated so their valuation will settle at the lower of the CCA closing valuation or their original purchase agreement valuation. These tokens are expected to become transferable at the same time as CCA tokens, after the cooldown period.

### Team and Advisors

Allocated to the core team and advisors who supported Interfold's early development.
